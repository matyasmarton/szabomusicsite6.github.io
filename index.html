About Us

The Bridge Between
Talent & Opportunity

Founded in Budapest, Szabo Music Company has been the premier connection between exceptional European musicians and the world's most prestigious cruise lines. Our deep understanding of both the music industry and maritime entertainment creates unparalleled opportunities for artists and cruise companies alike.

Miklos Szabo is a graduate of the Franz Liszt Academy of Music in Budapest, Hungary. He has made his mark as maritime musician performing for industry leading cruise lines, notably Holland America Line, Viking Ocean Cruises, P&O Cruises and Cunard Line. His decade long experience has taken him around the world and earned him a wealth of insights and connections in the music business. Miklos takes pride in overseeing the development of young musicians, arranging the right position for the right performer and providing professional management to clients.
